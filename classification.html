  • Classification

For classification, we train the Support Vector Machine (SVM) classifier using annotated training images. SVMs are versatile, serving not only binary classification but also multi-class objectives through one-to-one or one-to-all construction methods. During the training process, standardization is necessary to ensure that no special feature has a significant impact on the classification phase. Finally, we make predictions with the trained classifier by feeding unlabeled test images.
